Robot Arm Simulation using openGL

Camera: W / A / S / D to move

Joint controls :
Base yaw: Q , A 
Shoulder pitch: E , D
Elbow pitch: R , F 
Wrist pitch: T , G 

Reset pose: Z
Exit: Esc
